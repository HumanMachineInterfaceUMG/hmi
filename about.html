About us

HMI Research Club - “From sea waves to radio waves” is the main slogan of our club, because we have specialists in low and high voltages, programmers, enthusiasts of electronic equipment, as well as future mechanical officers and ETOs. We participate in conferences and scientific seminars, win hackathons, and obtain grants for large scientific projects. We are united by a wide range of interests, which we develop in many technical areas. The club's supervisor is Dr. Monika Rybczak from the Department of Autonomous Systems.
